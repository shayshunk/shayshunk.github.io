Geometry Modeling

To answer design questions for the proposed PROSPECT-II detector, I built a parameterized GEANT4 model. Simple options allow the users to construct any array of optical segments they wish. Easily modifiable geometrical parameters are given to accommodate different design parameters, such as window spacing and PMT housing thickness, with the detector resizing itself seamlessly to fit any user options.
